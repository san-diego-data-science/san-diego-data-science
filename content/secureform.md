### What is secureform.dev?

Secureform.dev converts your existing forms (like PDFs or paper forms) into modern, mobile-first digital forms. It streamlines data collection and management by integrating submissions directly into tools your team already uses.

### How it works

1.  **Transformation:** You upload your current form (e.g., a PDF).
2.  **Digitalization:** We transform it into an online digital form optimized for all devices.
3.  **Automation:** All submissions are automatically organized in a **Google Sheet**.
4.  **Notifications:** You receive email notifications for every new response.

### Benefits

*   **No Technical Setup:** We handle all hosting, maintenance, security, and uptime.
*   **Familiar Tools:** Your staff continues to use Google Sheets to review and manage submissions, eliminating manual data entry.
*   **AI-Powered Insights:** Includes an AI dashboard that allows non-technical users to gain insights from responses using plain English queries.
*   **Production-Ready:** We ensure your forms are always available and secure.

### Powered by Cloudflare

Secureform.dev leverages **Cloudflare's** world-class infrastructure to provide superior security, reliability, and performance. This ensures that your data collection is always protected and your forms are blazing fast for users worldwide.
